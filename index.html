About Us

We are a virtual reality and gaming center in Boise, Idaho, located on the corner of Orchard and Overland, next door to the liquor store. Our prices are per minute, rather than per hour. This gives our customers the freedom to play as little or long as they want, without being interrupted or cut off. Our customers' comfort and freedom to enjoy the experience are very important to us.
